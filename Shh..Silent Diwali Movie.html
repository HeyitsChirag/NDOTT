"Shh..Silent Diwali" Repesents the occasion of diwali which is celebrated in india where all the people burns cracker's where as this short film shows us the cons of burning the crackers and showing the beautiful path of celebrating the occasion without using the cracker's.
It is a well made short film with great cinematography, using the background music greatfully and at the end a a voice-over telling about how to celebrate diwali occaasion with using the fire crackers..This short film is suitable for all the age group, "Shh..Silent Diwali" is a must-see film that tells us about the diwali occaasion and how should we celebrate it!!...
